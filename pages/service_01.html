Top Manpower Staffing Services in Saudi Arabia

Established in 2005, Pearl Lina has become a trusted name in recruitment solutions within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia (KSA). We specialize in connecting local and foreign companies with top-tier talent from Asia, Europe, and the USA, particularly for businesses based in KSA. Our expertise spans multiple industries, including Oil and Gas, Construction, Sales and Marketing, Hospitality, Software and IT, Aviation, and Finance and Accounting. We are dedicated to fulfilling your manpower needs, ensuring that you have the skilled workforce necessary to drive your business forward.

Why Choose Pearl Lina for Your Recruitment Needs?

Industry Expertise

At Pearl Lina, we understand that each industry has its unique challenges and requirements. Our team of experienced consultants possesses in-depth knowledge of various sectors, allowing us to tailor our recruitment strategies to meet the specific needs of your business. Whether you are in Oil and Gas, Construction, Sales and Marketing, Hospitality, Software and IT, Aviation, or Finance and Accounting, we have the expertise to find the right talent for you.

Comprehensive Recruitment Solutions

We offer a wide range of recruitment services designed to meet the diverse needs of businesses in KSA. Our services include:

  • Recruitment Services: We specialize in sourcing and placing skilled manpower, office executives, managerial personnel, and C-level staffing across various industries.
  • Employer of Record (EOR) Services: Our EOR services allow you to hire employees without the need to establish a local entity, handling all compliance, payroll, and HR responsibilities.
  • Outsourced Payroll Services: We manage payroll processes, ensuring accurate and timely payments while handling tax compliance and other payroll-related tasks.
  • Visa Services: We assist with the visa application process, ensuring compliance with local regulations and facilitating a smooth transition for your international hires.
  • Aqama and Almahina Services: Our team provides expert guidance in obtaining residency and work permits for foreign workers in KSA.
  • HR Services: We offer comprehensive HR solutions, including talent management, employee relations, performance management, and training and development.

Global Talent Pool

With our extensive network across Asia, Europe, and the USA, we have access to a vast pool of qualified candidates. We leverage our global reach to find the best talent for your projects, ensuring that you have the right people in place to drive your operations forward.

Our Recruitment Process

Step 1: Consultation

We begin with a thorough consultation to understand your specific recruitment needs, company culture, and operational requirements. This helps us tailor our services to meet your expectations.

Step 2: Talent Sourcing

Using our extensive network and advanced recruitment tools, we source candidates from various regions, ensuring a diverse and qualified talent pool.

Step 3: Screening and Selection

Our rigorous screening process includes interviews, skills assessments, and background checks to ensure that we present you with the best candidates.

Step 4: Onboarding

Once you select your candidates, we assist with the onboarding process, ensuring a smooth transition into your organization. Our team provides support in integrating new hires into your company culture and ensuring they have the resources they need to succeed.

Step 5: Ongoing Support

Our commitment to your success extends beyond recruitment. We provide ongoing support, including HR services and compliance management, to ensure your workforce remains engaged and productive. We are here to address any challenges that may arise and to help you adapt to the evolving needs of your business.

Industry-Specific Recruitment Solutions

1. Oil and Gas

The oil and gas industry is a critical sector in KSA, requiring specialized skills and expertise. We understand the unique challenges of this industry, from exploration to production and refining. Our recruitment services focus on sourcing skilled engineers, geologists, project managers, and safety professionals who can contribute to your operations.

2. Construction

With KSA's ambitious infrastructure projects, the construction industry is booming. We specialize in recruiting skilled labor, project managers, site supervisors, and safety officers who can ensure that your projects are completed on time and within budget. Our understanding of local regulations and industry standards allows us to find the right talent for your construction needs.

3. Sales and Marketing

In a competitive market, having a strong sales and marketing team is essential for success. We help you find talented sales representatives, marketing managers, brand strategists, and digital marketing specialists who can drive your business growth. Our recruitment strategies focus on identifying candidates with the right skills and cultural fit for your organization.

4. Hospitality

The hospitality industry in KSA is rapidly growing, driven by increased tourism and investment. We specialize in recruiting front-of-house staff, culinary professionals, event coordinators, and hotel managers who can deliver exceptional guest experiences. Our understanding of the hospitality sector allows us to find candidates who are passionate about service excellence.

5. Software and IT

As technology continues to evolve, the demand for skilled IT professionals is on the rise. We provide recruitment solutions for software developers, system analysts, network engineers, and cybersecurity experts. Our focus is on sourcing candidates who not only possess the technical skills but also align with your company’s vision and culture.

6. Aviation

The aviation industry is a vital part of KSA's economy, and we are committed to supporting its growth by providing recruitment services for pilots, air traffic controllers, maintenance engineers, and ground staff. Our expertise in this sector ensures that we can meet the specific needs of airlines and aviation companies.

7. Finance and Accounting

In the finance and accounting sector, having the right talent is crucial for compliance and strategic decision-making. We specialize in recruiting accountants, financial analysts, auditors, and tax specialists who can help your organization navigate the complexities of financial management.
